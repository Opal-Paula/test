What is this site (about)?
"Book A Thought" is about how you, as a reader, get involved with the plot of a book.
Ever had any thoughts on it?
Like "I want to be that character!", "I can build a dragon house too!" or "if I had those powers I would..." or just simply recreate the plot as you would want to happen ("team Cersei for the win!").
Why am I here?
Because you are a bookaholick, a thinker, an otaku or just someone who appreciates a good read.
What if I don't read books?
"Book" here is used as a general meaning.
Example of types of books:

Good old plain paper book Game books/stories Ebook Comics or mangas Newspapers

Will I find something interesting?
Well you scrolled this far so maybe the answer is Yes.
What is with the random dots that I see near some of the words?
These mark the random thoughts the author is having. Put it simply, like an old'man's rambling. Also blue is for thoughts and, on hover, yellow is for reading thoughts.
